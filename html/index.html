Loanwords in Rabbinic Literature

This is a page about the loanwords occurring in two major works of the Rabbinic literature, the midrash Genesis Rabbah and the Yalkut Shimoni. Here you find an examination of the predominantly Greek borrowed vocabulary in both works, written in Rabbinic Hebrew and Aramaic.
